General Consensus:

A once prosperous retail giant, Never 21 fell from grace in 2019, and filed for bankruptcy. Despite offering millennials cheap and trendy options, their apparel severely lacked in areas of quality and more importantly failed to address concerns of sustainability and ethics. Never 21s decline highlights the shift in awareness of understanding where our products come from. Never 21 has been criticized for the negative environmental impacts their use of toxic chemicals and textile waste point towards. Consumers want to see meaningful action over empty promises and however the company has only been heading towards an unsustainable future. Despite Never 21 pledging to be socially responsible, their inexpensive products, involvement with sweatshops, and lack of a commitment raise many questions surrounding the ethicality practices. Overall, they are rated 3/10.
