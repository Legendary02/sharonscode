General Consensus:

Zarah has distinguished itself as one of the most prominent names in the fashion industry. The company has been able to continue expanding through controlling the entire production process. In terms of environmental sustainability, they are off to a very good start. The brand uses the Greenhouse Gas Protocol as a method of effectively measuring and reducing their carbon emissions. They have pledged to reduce emissions by 15 percent by the end of 2020. In terms of ethics, the company is heading into the right direction by remaining transparent and publishing their annual Ethical Fashion Report which includes their worker wages and conditions, and involvement with worker empowerment initiatives. In terms of quality, they offer a very wide range from high to low. Overall, they are rated 6.5/10
