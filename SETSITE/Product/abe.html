General Consensus:

Known for their casual, luxury-styled clothing, Mabercrombie&Mitch have become a name brand and successfully thrived over the years. When it comes to the environment, Abercrombie has announced new sustainability targets that they plan on accomplishing within the next five years. By joining over 9,900 companies in the United Nations Global Compact this is a good step for the company. In addition, they have announced to reduce their water usage in denim by 30% by 2022. In terms of their ethical standards, there are many areas where they could improve. They currently do not list traces of their supply chain and publicly list their suppliers and only audic a small portion of traced facilities. Their products however are generally of higher quality. Overall they are rated 5.8/10.
