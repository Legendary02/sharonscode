Our Mission

Style Sustainably strives to educate others on the impacts that factors such as fast fashion have on our environment and society.
We aim to, through our four values, promote a less wasteful and ethical future in the fashion industry. Change starts through small actions.
Together, with the help of one another and improved communication, we can create lasting impacts!

  • EDUCATION

  • SUSTAINABLILITY

  • ETHICS

  • QUALITY

Education

While we can't force anyone to start building their wardrobe with more ethical and sustainable pieces,
we can use the power of education to provide facts.
Shoppers can use this information as they need, and we hope through educating more people, they will feel
inclined to make more conscious shopping decisions.
Back to Our Mission

Sustainability

Fast fashion and textile waste can have detrimental impacts on our environment and economy.
It promotes huge wastes of water, uses of unnatural dyes, and water pollution.
We hope that through supporting more sustainable brands, we can support our local economy and take care of our planet.
Back to Our Mission

Ethics

We belive that all clothing companies should pay their workers fair wages and take care of them.
Unfortunately, this is not the case for many large corporations. There have been reports of child labour, dangerous workplaces, etc.
We want to discontinue our support for companies who unfaily treat workers and stand for fair trade.
Back to Our Mission

Quality

The quality of clothing can have impacts on their durability. We try to reduce clothing and textile waste,
so choosing items made of good quality items can extend the wear of our clothes
Moreover, we aim to explore textiles that are repurposed, reused, etc.
