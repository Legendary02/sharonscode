Who are We?

Style Sustainably is a student-led project that aims to reduce clohing waste and improve ethics behind certain practices. Moreover, Style Sustainably strives to create environmental and economic sustainability in the fashion industry, through promoting local business and discouraging the fast fashion wasteful trends. Inspired by the UN's SDGs, we aim to create sustainability in our local communities, encourage responsible consumption and production, and take care of our planet!

   

Join the New Trend

How can you help with our goal? Together, let's start being more conscious of the clothing we purchase and educate ourselves about the brands they come from. Moreover, let's educate ourselves about how these items were made, their materials and whether or not they were ethically sourced. When we don't wear our clothes anymore, consider repurposing! For more ideas on what to do with your old clothes, visit our repurposing ideas.
